Who are STEMgirls?

We are three girls who completed the Codefirst: Girls beginners course and wanted to create a site to help more girls into STEM, particularly those age 16-18 making key choices about their future career paths.

What is STEMgirls?

STEMgirls is a website to help young females find information, inspiration and opportunities for careers in Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths. For more information check out our stories page.

Why is it important to get girls into STEM?

Women are under-represented in STEM fields, and girls are far less likely to choose STEM subjects for their GCSE's, A-Levels and degrees. This means the workforce is not diverse enough, and does not represent women properly. It also means women and girls who would thrive in a STEM-based career are missing out because they think STEM is not for girls. We're working hard to change that!

How can I get involved with STEMgirls?

We are always looking for new contributors for our stories page and for the central team. If you have a coding story or opportunity to share please do here!
