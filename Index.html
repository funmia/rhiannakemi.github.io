What is STEMgirls?

STEMgirls is a website to help young females find information, inspiration and opportunities for careers in Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths. For more information go to the Stories page


Why is it important to get girls into STEM?

We are three girls who completed the Codefirst: Girls beginners course and wanted to create a site to help more girls into STEM, particularly those age 16-18 making key choices about their future career paths.

How can I get involved with STEMgirls?

We are always looking for new contributors for our stories page and for the central team. If you have a coding story or opportunity to share please do!
